At A Glance... LJ Knowledge Foundation

LJ Knowledge Foundation has been set up to create awareness and the understanding of innovation, entrepreneurship and intellectual property rights (IPR) among the student communities through creation of knowledge resources, pioneering new pedagogies and academic pilots for teaching innovation/start-ups/IPR. It is an initiative that works towards fostering innovation and entrepreneurship in students pursuing professional programmes in areas such as engineering, pharmacy, management and computer applications. LJ Knowledge Foundation is a not-for-profit organization. Its activities focus on creation of innovative knowledge products, innovative systems and services, and resulting in greater good of the society and the nation. LJ Knowledge Foundation has inked an MOU with SIM Global Education, Singapore and we will jointly launch an International Centre for Entrepreneurship development, where faculty exchange programmes and FDPs will be part of it. Joint programmes and FDPs with TiE, HeadStart Network and other industry networks in the ecosystem. DPs and leadership programmes on entrepreneurship to faculty members at IIM Ahmedabad.

LJ Knowledge Foundation with an aim of promoting and inculcating the spirit of innovation and entrepreneurship had sensitized more than 15000 students and 300 faculty members

Objectives

  • To make patent analysis, which has already been started in 2012, a regular co-curricular activity for the third year students of degree pharmacy and degree engineering to enable them to take up innovative projects for their curricular requirements.
  • Encourage students to solve real life problems being faced by the common man, rural and the underprivileged class
  • Inspire young students to take up entrepreneurship and nurturing their entrepreneurial initiatives
  • Work towards improving the educational pedagogy in Indian educational institutes by making available in public domain new multimedia resources on teaching
  • To facilitate students in getting their patents registered by providing necessary administrative, technical and funding support.
  • To train human resources for teaching and guiding innovation and IPR.
  • To undertake such other activities which may lead to creation of innovative knowledge products, innovative systems and services, and resulting in greater good of the society and the nation
  • To create end to end support system for promoting innovation & entrepreneurship inside and outside of LJ campus.
  • To play a critical role in developing local and national innovation & start-up ecosystem involving youth.
