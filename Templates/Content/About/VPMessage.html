Vice-President’s Message


Dr. Manish Shah
Vice-President, LJK

India is a young nation with vast pool of young students across universities. Given a platform these young students can turn to innovate and solve challenges of the country. Every team in their academic program does a project/thesis as requirement of their academic program. While huge amount of energy is being spent by students on their projects, platforms are rare where they can showcase their innovation and supports can be linked to them to take their ideas ahead. We at LJ are keen to create an end to end ecosystem where ideas, innovation & possible student start-ups are getting nurtured at different stages so that their creative potential is harnessed effectively.


LJ Innovation Village is a platform for young student innovators, start-ups to showcase their creative potential in front of stakeholders in the campus, industry experts, mentors, policy makers, investors and others. In past five editions over 1000 student innovations have been showcased through this platform. From this edition onwards we will open up this platform for all student innovators across the nation. LJ Innovation Village 2018 will have innovations across domain and states who will showcase their solutions/innovations to all.


LJ Innovation Village is not only a platform to showcase innovations but also we are trying to link various support systems with innovators through this. All innovators will get access to industry mentors, designers, market experts and other resources available through our campus incubation facility. We will see that best innovations also get seed grants so that they can take their innovation/project to next stage. We are committed to see that at least the best ones reach to cater the need of end users so that innovation to impact journey is covered end to end. LJ Innovation Village will also help the innovators to protect their IPR and file patents for their novel works. We will also provide access to our auxiliary ecosystem where innovators can fetch extended benefits from variety of stakeholders.


Some of our earlier exhibits have already taken their innovation to market. I am confident that this edition will also multiply such impact. Over 1000 students and aspiring innovators will also visit the innovation pavilion this year. We will have dozen parallel sessions involving various experts where innovators can benefit apart from exhibition. I am sure LJ Innovation Village 2018 will set new benchmark towards making attempt to support early stage innovations and contribute significantly towards the national attempt like “Make in India”, “Start-up India”.
