LJ Women Entrepreneurship Development Programme (LJWEDP)

It is an initiative by LJK Foundation in order to bring up the entrepreneurial potential of women. This is a five week workshop, with an aim of supporting and developing the skills of women and giving them equal opportunities in the field of entrepreneurship.Antrapreneur-The Business Incubator conducted the Entrepreneurship Development Programme to bring about the hidden zest, immensely potential qualities and the confidence of becoming an entrepreneur within the students and faculties in the LJ Campus. The programme focused on encouraging, empowering and educating the potential women entrepreneurs in all areas of practical relevance to entrepreneurs including Entrepreneurial Orientation, Business Planning, Economic Environment, Business Economics, Industry Analysis, Business Strategy, Product Development, Marketing Sales, Service Marketing, Product Management, Operations & Supply Chain Management, Accounting, Costing, Finance, Banking, Law, Taxation, Communication, Negotiation and Networking.

In an effort to encourage, empower and educate potential women entrepreneurs to create their own business or social enterprise, as well as to enable women entrepreneurs to grow their existing Enterprises, LJ Group of Institutes is conducting Women Entrepreneurship Development Programme (WEDP) for Women Entrepreneurs across Gujarat at the LJK campus every year for 14 days where more than 100 female entrepreneurs and professionals across the nation participates as part of this event. 1000 Women Entrepreneurs is a global initiative initiated by LJ Group of Institutes which intends to provide women entrepreneurs across Gujarat a platform with practical business education, business advise, and networking.
