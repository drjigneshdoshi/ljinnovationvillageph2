Entrepreneur Development

Startup Gujarat, Standup Gujarat

LJ Knowledge Foundation launched the Startup campaign “Startup Gujarat Standup Gujarat” on 26/09/205, and also felicitated major stakeholders of the Gujarat Ecosystem for their valuable efforts towards the development of Gujarat Start-up Ecosystem in their own personal and institutional capacity. Honorable, Education Minister, Shri. Bhupendrasinh Chudasama was the Chief Guest of the program at LJ Campus on 26th September, 2015.

Around 50 Start-ups and Start-up enthusiasts from Gujarat also joined this program. Around 200 start-up aspirants also attended this grand event who had attended various entrepreneurship exposure programs earlier.

A vibrant start-up ecosystem needs co-creation through all complementary actors across the innovation and start-up value chain. This occasion emphasized on need of coming together and developing strategies and platforms to support young start-ups and strengthen the knowledge economy.
